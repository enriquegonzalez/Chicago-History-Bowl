Chicago Authors Wins!

We asked Chicago to give us an assignment, and we are excited to present Chicago Authors as a future exhibition at the Museum.
— Museum President Gary T. Johnson

In the final round of voting, for the Chicago History Bowl, Chicago Authors beat out Prohibition Era and The Roaring 20's, Neighborhoods, and Architecture as a future exhibition topic at the Museum.

Now our work begins. If you have any thoughts or suggestions regarding Chicago Authors, please send them to x@chicagohistory.org.
